Common Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers

Is the initial consultation really free?
+
Yes, absolutely. Your first consultation is completely free — no charges, no commitments. We evaluate your profile, understand your goals and give you an honest overview of your options.
Which countries do you help students apply to?
+
We support applications to the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Germany, Ireland, Netherlands, Japan, UAE and more. Contact us for specific country guidance tailored to your profile.
Do I need IELTS or TOEFL to study abroad?
+
Most English-speaking countries require proof of English proficiency. However, some universities offer conditional admissions or waivers. We will advise you based on your specific situation and target country.
How long does the entire process take?
+
It varies by country and intake, but typically the full process from first consultation to visa approval takes 3 to 8 months. We recommend starting early to explore all options and meet deadlines comfortably.
Can you help with scholarship applications?
+
Yes. We guide students through identifying merit-based and need-based scholarships, government grants and university-specific financial aid. We help prepare strong applications to maximize your funding chances.
What documents do I need to start?
+
Generally you will need academic transcripts, a valid passport, English proficiency test scores, statement of purpose and letters of recommendation. We provide a complete personalized checklist during your consultation.
